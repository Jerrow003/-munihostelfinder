Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to register to view hostels?

No, you can browse all hostels without registration. However, you need to register and login to view contact details and make bookings.

How do I book a hostel?

Register as a student, login, then click "View Details" on any hostel. You'll see a "Book Now" button if you're logged in.

What information do hostel owners see?

Hostel administrators can only see their own hostel information and bookings. They cannot see other hostels or student details.

How do I become a hostel admin?

Register as a hostel admin, then contact the system administrator to assign you a hostel and activate your account.
